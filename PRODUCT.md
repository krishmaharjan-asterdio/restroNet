# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Food Enthusiasts & Urban Explorers**: Looking for quality dining, trending spots, and seamless booking.
- **Restaurant Owners/Managers**: Needing efficient oversight of reservations, visibility, and engagement.

## Product Purpose
RestroNet serves as a curated culinary bridge in Kathmandu, enabling high-intent discovery for diners and operational excellence for restaurant owners. Success is a booked table and a delighted diner.

## Brand Personality
- **Modern**: Clean lines, contemporary layouts, and current tech affordances.
- **Local**: Deeply rooted in the Kathmandu dining scene; feels familiar yet elevated.
- **Effortless**: High utility with low friction. The UI should "get out of the way."

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS**: Avoid overly corporate, cold, or "boxed" layouts.
- **Cluttered Directories**: No noisy ad-filled grids or information overload.
- **Gimmicky UX**: Avoid excessive gradients, bounce animations, or "AI-slop" patterns.
- **Tailwind-Template Look**: Avoid the generic startup aesthetic; prioritize curation and intention.

## Design Principles
- **Curation over Collection**: Every element should feel picked, not just placed.
- **Functional Elegance**: Beauty must serve a purpose; polish should never hinder speed.
- **Intentional Spacing**: Use white space to create a "lifestyle" feel on the customer side.
- **Operational Clarity**: High-density information for admins must remain readable and actionable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**: High contrast for readability.
- **Responsive-First**: Seamless experience across mobile (discovery) and desktop (management).
